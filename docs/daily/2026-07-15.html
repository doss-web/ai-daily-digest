📄 重要论文

发生了什么
该论文提出 PUST（Proxy-guided Update Signal Transfer），将后训练中的策略探索与分布对齐解耦，允许在大模型上执行一个小的代理模型进行低成本探索，然后将优化信号（奖励模型等）迁移给更大的目标模型。实验显示，PUST 在多个下游任务上以更低开销实现了与完整 RLHF 相当的性能。
为什么重要
当前的 RLHF / RLVR 需要在每个新模型上重新运行昂贵的 rollout。PUST 的“训练一次、迁移多次”思路如果可扩展，将显著降低持续后训练的成本。对计划频繁更新模型的研究团队和公司来说非常重要。

发生了什么
提出 KronQ，一种后训练量化框架，引入了梯度协方差矩阵的 Kronecker 近似来构建更准确的量化目标。相比只利用激活统计的 GPTQ 等二阶方法，KronQ 考虑了不同输出通道的重要性差异。在多个 LLM 和不同比特设置下，KronQ 在困惑度和下游任务上均优于现有方法。
为什么重要
量化是部署大模型的关键步骤。KronQ 提供了一个计算开销可控但增益显著的新方法，可以直接替代当前主流的 GPTQ / AWQ。对于需要将 70B+ 模型部署到有限硬件上的团队，值得关注实验。

发生了什么
该研究探索了“弱到强”后训练方法：在轻量模型上运行 RLVR（可验证奖励强化学习），然后将学到的知识蒸馏给更强的目标模型。实验证明，通过精心设计的在线策略蒸馏（on-policy distillation），弱模型经过 RL 训练后的输出可以有效提升强模型的推理能力，且成本远低于在强模型上直接做 RL。
为什么重要
直接挑战了“越大越好”的 RL 范式。如果弱到强泛化能成为可靠方法，那么后训练的计算开销可以降低数倍，尤其利好中小团队——他们可以用较小模型探索，然后将其能力赋予更大模型。

📄 Papers

What happened
This paper proposes PUST (Proxy-guided Update Signal Transfer), which decouples policy exploration from distribution alignment in post-training. It enables cheap exploration on a small proxy model, then transfers optimization signals (reward models, etc.) to a larger target model. Experiments show PUST achieves performance comparable to full RLHF at a fraction of the cost.
Why it matters
Current RLHF/RLVR requires expensive rollout on every new model. PUST's "train once, transfer many" approach, if scalable, could dramatically reduce the cost of continuous post-training. Especially important for research teams and companies that frequently update models.
What happened
KronQ is a post-training quantization framework that introduces Kronecker approximation of the gradient covariance matrix to build more accurate quantization objectives. Unlike second-order methods like GPTQ that only use activation statistics, KronQ accounts for differing importance across output channels. KronQ outperforms existing methods in perplexity and downstream tasks across multiple LLMs and bit settings.
Why it matters
Quantization is critical for deploying large models. KronQ offers a computationally affordable method with significant gains, potentially replacing GPTQ/AWQ. Teams needing to deploy 70B+ models on limited hardware should evaluate it.
What happened
This research explores a weak-to-strong post-training method: run RLVR on a lightweight model, then distill the learned knowledge to a stronger target model.
