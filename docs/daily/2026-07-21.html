📄 重要论文

发生了什么
作者提出 Self-Verified Reasoner (SVR-R1)，一个多轮 RL 框架，让模型将自己的验证结果作为学习信号。模型首先生成答案，然后自判正确/错误；若判错则触发重思考，最终答案用于计算奖励。无需外部验证器，使用 GRPO 实现。
为什么重要
这突破了传统 RL（如 GRPO）只奖励最终答案的限制，让模型学会在推理过程中自我修正。对于提升多模态推理的鲁棒性很有价值，可直接应用于需要可靠推理的 Agent 系统。

📄 Papers

What happened
The authors propose Self-Verified Reasoner (SVR-R1), a multi-turn RL framework that turns the model's own verification into a learning signal. The model generates an answer, issues a binary self-verdict (Yes/No), and triggers a rethink if "No." The final answer computes outcome-based reward — no external verifier needed, implemented with GRPO.
Why it matters
This breaks the limitation of traditional RL (e.g., GRPO) that only rewards the final answer, teaching the model to self-correct during reasoning. Highly valuable for improving robustness of multimodal reasoning in agent systems.
What happened
Agon pits two competing models against each other to grade each other's solutions. On the same problem, one acts as solver while the other reads and solves simultaneously to provide a rating. Roles alternate, creating mutual supervision signals that address the "write more ≠ think better" problem.
Why it matters
Agon provides a finer-grained, no-human-label supervision signal that improves reasoning process quality. For teams aiming for "reasoning well" rather than "writing long," this is a novel training paradigm.
What happened
This paper systematically studies how pretraining choices (model size, data) shape returns from RL post-training, and what RL actually does to the model. Using controlled math tasks, they answer "why some models improve greatly with RL while others don't."
Why it matters
Foundational understanding for any LLM post-training work — pretraining decisions determine the ceiling of RL efficiency. Teams doing pretraining should consider data quality and model size's fit to downstream RL.
What happened
The authors release the Loopie series — two MoE models (20B params/2B active and 6B/0.6B). Looped Transformers have long suffered from "looping N times underperforms adding N× parameters." Loopie overcomes this with extensive ablations and architectural improvements, proving that looping can beat parameter scaling.
Why it matters
A viable looped Transformer means deeper reasoning with fewer active parameters, offering huge potential for reducing inference cost and memory. The ablation study provides clear guidance for future looped architecture design.
What happened
Xiaomi released Xiaomi-Robotics-1, a VLA foundation model pretrained on 100,000 hours of real-world mobile manipulation trajectories. It can follow diverse language instructions to perform unseen tasks out of the box and efficiently adapt to new tasks with minimal fine-tuning.
Why it matters
Demonstrates feasibility of large-scale real robot data training with cross-scenario generalization. For robotics researchers and engineers, this is a valuable pretrained model that could lower the barrier to developing robot applications.
