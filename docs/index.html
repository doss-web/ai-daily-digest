The clearest signal today is the full-scale eruption of the **"model supply chain trust crisis."** Nadella publicly criticizing OpenAI/Anthropic's "reverse information paradox," Apple suing OpenAI for stealing hardware secrets, and S&P listing OpenAI as a credit risk for Oracle—three major events pointing in the same direction: enterprise AI dependencies are evolving from a technical choice to a strategic risk.
