💡 Today's Take

The strongest signal today is the **acceleration of "de-NVIDIA-fication" in AI infrastructure**: OpenAI's custom Jalapeño inference chip, Qualcomm's acquisition of chip software startup Modular, and DFlash's 15x throughput on Blackwell all point to AI compute shifting from "buying GPUs" to "custom silicon + software co-optimization." Meanwhile, **open-source model cost-performance is putting real pressure on closed-source** — GLM-5.2 approaches Claude Opus 4.7 at one-fifth the cost, while Qwen-AgentWorld and OpenThoughts-Agent make open-source contributions to agent training paradigms. Finally, **agent security and compliance have become unavoidable** — Meta's employee data leak and NVIDIA's SkillSpector launch remind the industry that agent deployment security auditing must keep pace with capability growth.
